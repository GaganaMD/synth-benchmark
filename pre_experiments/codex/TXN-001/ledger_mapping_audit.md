- true mapping: source_purchase_ledger is present, recommended_ledger matches it, and rationale says the ledger exists in chart_of_accounts.csv.
- inferred mapping: source ledger absent, but a nonblank ledger is recommended from historical/COA/recurring evidence.
- assumed mapping: no reliable source/inference evidence supports a nonblank assignment, or the result remains blank/ambiguous.
